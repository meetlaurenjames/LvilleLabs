Privacy Policy for Keystorm: Typing Game

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Keystorm: Typing Game (the “App”) is developed and maintained by Lville Labs LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect certain information when you use the App, including usage data such as gameplay activity, typing speed, accuracy, session behavior, and app interaction data, including crash reports. We may also collect device-related information such as device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and IP address for analytics and fraud prevention purposes.

In addition, we use Google Firebase Analytics to better understand how users interact with the App. This may include information such as session duration, screens viewed, and feature usage.

2. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, including device identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, and information about ad interactions. This information is used to display relevant advertisements, measure performance, and prevent fraudulent activity.

More information about how Google uses data can be found at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads .

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to improve app performance and user experience, analyze gameplay trends and usage patterns, display relevant advertisements, and identify, fix, and prevent technical issues.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. However, we may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers, including Google Firebase for analytics and crash reporting, and Google AdMob for advertising services. These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You may limit or disable personalized advertising through your device settings. Depending on your device, you may also limit ad tracking through iOS or Android system settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted within the App or on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at info@lvillelabs.com. Lville Labs LLC is responsible for the operation of the App.

10. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of data associated with their use of Keystorm: Typing Game by contacting us at info@lvillelabs.com.

Because Keystorm does not require user accounts, we do not store personal profiles. However, limited data may exist through third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics.

Upon request, we will work with applicable providers where possible to facilitate deletion or anonymization of associated data. Requests are typically processed within 30 days.
